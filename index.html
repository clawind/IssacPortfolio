Robotics & Innovation

The First Lego League is more than just robots; it’s about solving real-world problems. As the Captain of the Brick Beasts (Team 71237) during the Unearthed 2025-2026 season, I led my team to the First Lego League State Competition, where we earned the First Place Core Values Award. Our project focused on using 3D scanners to replicate and decipher artifacts, allowing history to be shared across the world instantly. By utilizing this creative 3D scanning technology to virtually transport historical artifacts, we worked to make global heritage accessible to everyone. To bring our technical ideas to life, we wrote and performed an engaging skit that demonstrated the real-world impact of our research, showcasing our ability to combine engineering excellence with effective communication and teamwork.
